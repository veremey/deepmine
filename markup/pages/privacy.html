Privacy Policy

Mineralt Script Privacy Policy

MinerAlt offers a JavaScript miner for the Monero Blockchain (Why Monero?) that you can embed in your website. Your users run the miner directly in their Browser and mine XMR for you in turn for an ad-free experience, in-game currency or whatever incentives you can come up with.

Site Privacy

If you have a Mineralt account, your email address, password and payment information will be stored on our servers. This information never leaves our servers. We will not disclose your email address to any third party. Your account passwords are stored hashed and salted using Bcrypt.

We record your IP address with your account information with each login into your Mineralt account. It is used to reproduce what happened in case your account was compromised.

We collect analytics data on our site.

We use cookies to distinguish you from other users of our website.

We also use the localStorage API.

We also use Google Analytics, please see its terms here: https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/

This list is complete and actual. It may be changed in the future. Mineralt is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.
